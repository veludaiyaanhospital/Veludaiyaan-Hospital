# Veludaiyaan Hospital SEO Checklist

This repository now includes technical SEO improvements. To maximize ranking for hospital-related searches, complete these off-site actions as well.

## 1) Google Search Console (Required)
- Add property: `https://www.veludaiyaanhospital.com/`
- Submit sitemap: `https://www.veludaiyaanhospital.com/sitemap.xml`
- Use URL Inspection and request indexing for:
  - `/`
  - `/about/`
  - `/services/`
  - `/doctors/`
  - `/facilities/`
  - `/contact/`
- Monitor Coverage, Core Web Vitals, and Manual Actions weekly.

## 2) Bing Webmaster Tools (Required)
- Add and verify the same domain.
- Submit the sitemap URL.
- Confirm IndexNow submissions are being received.

## 3) Google Business Profile (Critical for local ranking)
- Claim and fully verify the profile.
- Ensure exact NAP consistency:
  - Name: Veludaiyaan Ortho & Trauma Specialty Hospital
  - Address: 100-C, Pondy Road (Near Manikundu), Manjakuppam, Cuddalore - 607001
  - Phone: +91 78459 27606
- Add correct categories, services, photos, and emergency availability.
- Publish weekly updates and reply to every review.

## 4) Local Citation Consistency
- Keep identical hospital name, address, and phone on:
  - Google Business Profile
  - Bing Places
  - Apple Maps
  - Justdial / Practo / relevant local directories

## 5) Authority Signals
- Request backlinks from:
  - Nearby clinics and labs
  - Local medical associations
  - Cuddalore community portals/news listings
- Publish doctor profiles and treatment explainers regularly.

## 6) Monthly SEO Routine
- Add at least 2 new useful pages/articles per month.
- Refresh titles/descriptions where CTR is low in Search Console.
- Track top queries and improve matching sections on key pages.
